1. What Are Cookies?

This Cookies Policy explains what cookies are, how Clario Consulting Corporation uses them on the Clergy Housing platform, the types of cookies we use, and how you can manage your cookie preferences.

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website or web application. These files enable the platform to function properly, maintain your authenticated session securely, and help us understand how the platform is being used so we can improve it.

Clergy Housing is hosted on Amazon Web Services (AWS) infrastructure including AWS Amplify and uses AWS Cognito for user authentication. The cookies used by our platform reflect this AWS-based architecture. We do not use cookies for advertising or marketing purposes.

2. How We Use Cookies

The Clergy Housing platform uses both first-party and third-party cookies exclusively for the following purposes:

  • First-party cookies are set directly by the Clergy Housing platform and are essential for core functionality including user authentication, session management, and multi-factor authentication (MFA). They do not collect personally identifiable information beyond what is necessary to maintain your secure session.
  • Third-party cookies are set by our trusted service providers including AWS Cognito, Stripe, and Plaid. These cookies are used exclusively to facilitate secure authentication, payment processing, and bank account connectivity. They are never used for advertising, tracking, or marketing purposes.
Our commitment: We do not use cookies for advertising, behavioral tracking, or marketing purposes of any kind. No advertising networks, social media trackers, or analytics platforms that share data with third parties for marketing are used on the Clergy Housing platform.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

These cookies are essential for the Clergy Housing platform to function correctly and securely. They cannot be disabled without significantly impairing the platform's functionality. No personal financial data is stored in these cookies.

3.3 Bank Connectivity Cookies (Plaid)

4. Cookies We Do NOT Use

Clergy Housing is committed to protecting your privacy. We explicitly do not use the following types of cookies:

  • Advertising or marketing cookies of any kind
  • Social media tracking cookies (Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, Instagram)
  • Google advertising or remarketing cookies
  • YouTube or video platform tracking cookies
  • Cross-site behavioral tracking cookies
  • Third-party analytics cookies that share data with advertising networks
  • Any cookie from a platform or service not directly involved in delivering the Clergy Housing service

This commitment is consistent with our Privacy Policy, which states that your personal and financial data is never used for advertising, marketing, or any purpose beyond housing allowance expense tracking and tax report generation.

5. Cookie Consent

When you first visit the Clergy Housing platform, you will be presented with a cookie consent banner that explains the cookies we use and gives you the following options:

  • Accept All Cookies — accept all cookies including functional and analytics cookies
  • Necessary Cookies Only — accept only the strictly necessary cookies required for the platform to function
  • Manage Preferences — customize which categories of cookies you accept
Please note: Strictly necessary cookies including AWS Cognito session cookies and Stripe payment cookies cannot be disabled as they are required for the platform to function securely. Disabling these cookies will prevent you from logging in or processing subscription payments.

Your cookie consent preferences are saved in the clergy-housing-consent cookie for one year. You can change your preferences at any time by clicking the Cookie Settings link in the platform footer.

6. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

6.1 Platform Cookie Settings

You can manage your Clergy Housing cookie preferences at any time by clicking the Cookie Settings button in the platform footer. This will allow you to review, modify, or withdraw your cookie consent at any time.

6.2 Browser Settings

You can also manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to view, block, and delete cookies. Please note that blocking strictly necessary cookies will prevent you from using the Clergy Housing platform. Below are links to cookie management guides for the most commonly used browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Safari (Mac)
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Microsoft Edge

If you use a different browser, please refer to that browser's official support documentation for instructions on managing cookies.

7. Do Not Track Signals

Some browsers include a Do Not Track (DNT) feature that signals to websites that you do not want your online activities tracked. The Clergy Housing platform respects your privacy preferences. Because we do not use behavioral tracking or advertising cookies, enabling Do Not Track in your browser does not change the cookies used by our platform beyond what is already described in this policy.

The only cookies we use are those strictly necessary for platform operation, functional preferences, secure bank connectivity, and anonymous performance analytics — none of which involve cross-site behavioral tracking.

8. AWS Infrastructure and Security Cookies

The Clergy Housing platform is hosted entirely on Amazon Web Services (AWS) infrastructure in the United States. AWS may set certain security and performance cookies as part of its infrastructure services including:

  • AWS WAF (Web Application Firewall) tokens to protect against malicious traffic
  • AWS CloudFront cookies to optimize content delivery performance
  • AWS Cognito session tokens to manage secure authentication

These infrastructure cookies are strictly necessary for the secure and performant operation of the platform. They are governed by AWS's own privacy and security practices. For more information about AWS data handling, please visit aws.amazon.com/privacy.
