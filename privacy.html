1. Introduction

Clario Consulting Corporation ("Company", "we", "us", or "our") operates the Clergy Housing platform ("Platform", "Service") accessible at www.clergyhousing.com. We are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users' personal and financial information.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, protect, and share information about you when you use the Clergy Housing platform. Please read this policy carefully. By using the Clergy Housing platform, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

This Privacy Policy applies to all clergy members, ministers, pastors, and other ordained professionals ("users", "you") who register for and use the Clergy Housing platform.

5. How We Protect Your Information

We take the security of your personal and financial information seriously. The following security measures are in place to protect your data:

  • All data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption on AWS RDS and AWS S3
  • All data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.2 or higher on all platform connections
  • Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is required for all user accounts
  • Bank account credentials are never stored on our servers — handled exclusively by Plaid
  • All API keys and credentials are stored in AWS Secrets Manager and never exposed in code
  • Access to your data is restricted to authorized personnel on a strict need-to-know basis
  • AWS CloudTrail monitors all access to production systems and data
  • Automated vulnerability scanning and regular security reviews are conducted on all infrastructure

While we implement industry-standard security measures, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information. If you believe your account has been compromised, please contact us immediately at security@clergyhousing.com.

6.1 Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and all associated personal data at any time through the Settings screen or by contacting us at security@clergyhousing.com. Upon receiving a deletion request:

  • We will process your request within 30 days
  • All personal data will be permanently and irreversibly removed from all production systems, databases, and file storage
  • You will receive an email confirmation when your data has been deleted
  • We may retain certain information as required by law, for fraud prevention, or to resolve disputes

6.2 Automated Deletion Controls

The following automated deletion controls are enforced at the infrastructure level to ensure data is not retained beyond its scheduled retention period:

  • AWS RDS automated backup retention is configured to 30 days with automatic deletion thereafter
  • AWS S3 lifecycle policies enforce automatic deletion of expired temporary files and backups
  • Inactive accounts are automatically flagged for deletion after 90 days and users are notified by email before deletion occurs
  • Plaid access tokens are immediately revoked and deleted upon bank account disconnection
  • Session tokens expire automatically and are not retained beyond their active session

8. Plaid Bank Connectivity — Additional Disclosures

The Clergy Housing platform uses Plaid Technologies, Inc. to enable bank account connectivity. The following additional disclosures apply to the use of Plaid:

  • Plaid Link is used to securely connect your bank accounts — your bank credentials are entered directly into Plaid's secure interface and are never shared with Clergy Housing
  • By connecting a bank account, you authorize Clergy Housing to receive transaction data from Plaid for the purpose of identifying and importing qualifying housing allowance expenses
  • You may disconnect any linked bank account at any time by visiting the Bank Accounts screen in the Clergy Housing platform

9. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The Clergy Housing platform uses cookies to enable secure authentication, maintain your session, facilitate bank account connectivity through Plaid, and process subscription payments through Stripe. We do not use cookies for advertising, behavioral tracking, or marketing purposes of any kind.

Our cookies fall into the following categories:

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies — required for the platform to function securely, including AWS Cognito session management and Stripe payment processing cookies. These cannot be disabled.
  • Functional Cookies — used to remember your platform preferences and cookie consent settings between sessions.
  • Bank Connectivity Cookies — set by Plaid Technologies during the bank account connection process. These are session-only cookies that expire immediately after the connection process is complete.
  • Analytics Cookies — used to understand anonymous usage patterns to improve the platform. No personally identifiable financial information is included.

For complete details about the specific cookies we use, their duration, descriptions, and instructions for managing your cookie preferences, please review our full Cookies Policy at www.clergyhousing.com/cookies.

You may configure your browser to block cookies, but please note that blocking strictly necessary cookies will prevent you from logging in or using the Clergy Housing platform.
